Sentiment Analysis of Yik Yak

Sentiment Analysis of Yik Yak was a hackathon project that uses machine learning to determine the sentiment of Yaks (similar to tweets) on the top 150 college campuses around the United States. I built the visualization in d3 helping us to win the ManTech Prize for Best Use of an Undocumented Interface. Make sure to see the live site linked below and hover to see the sentiment values!

SmartBins

SmartBins was a hackathon project where we set out to solve the problem of not knowing the overhead bin capacity on a plane during boarding. Our solution was a hardware array that would be in the plane's overhead bins connecting via an arduino to a webpage built in d3 to show the real time bin capacity and total plane capacity.

We won Top 8 of over 1000 other students as well as Delta's first prize of Best Air Travel Solution

  • Live Site: SmartBins
  • Video Demo: YouTube
  • Devpost: SmartBins HackGT Devpost
  • HackGT 2016 - 36 Hour Hackathon
  • September 2016
  • Winner: Top 8, Best Air Travel Solutions

Cook Park Virtual Reality

I developed this virtual reality world for my class Technology in Sustainable Communities where we chose the final project and determined the success criteria. I chose to make a VR world of the proposed Cook Park so the residents of that area and anyone with access to a web browser would be able to look at what the park would look like and virutally walk around in it.

Use W S A D to walk around and click the mouse and drag to look around the park on desktop. Tap to walk and rotate your mobile device to look around. It is also compatable with phone VR headsets.

  • Live Site: Cook Park - Virtual Reality Park (May take a minute to load all the graphics)

Epix

Epix was my senior design project where I built a cross platform application in Electron to automatically create slideshows based on criteria of your choosing. For example you could open the map view and select photos taken in a certain country and then have a slideshow that just contains those photos. If you decide that you would like to add more photos, you can import them and not have to update any of the slideshows you have created.

Years Past

YearsPast is side project of mine I built during a winter break where I wanted to create a dynamic slideshow where I could just keep adding images to and would get a new slideshow every day.
